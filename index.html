How To Make A Camel

Facts about Camels

  1. There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  2. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  3. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.

How To Make A Cicada

Facts about Cicada

  • They Have One of the Longest Insect Life Spans. An annual cicada can live between two and five years, and a periodical cicada can live for up to 17 years in the larva stage.
  • Cicadas are large, stout insects.
  • Adults have four wings that they hold folded over their backs like the roof of a house.

How To Make A Panda

Facts about Panda

  • Pandas eat almost nothing but bamboo shoots and leaves.
  • Pandas have so many fans because they look cute.
  • An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!
